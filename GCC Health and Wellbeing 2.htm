 What is the Employee Assistance Programme?

Free, confidential, counselling and advice for staff, available 24 hours every day online and by telephone

GCC has invested in the wellbeing of all its employees and provides a Employee Assistance Programme (EAP) by Care first. This resource is available free of charge, offering immediate information, answers and advice to a range of workplace and personal issues. No matter how small or big the problem.

Expert advisors, trained by Citizens Advice, can provide comprehensive answers and assistance on a wide range of issues which affect daily life.

While Care first counsellors, accredited by the British association for Counselling and Psychotherapy , can offer support, advice and counselling on any workplace or personal related issue. Both one off and structured sessions are available.

Call 0800 174319 for 24 hour support and assistance

All calls are treated in strictest confidence and no information about calls is passed back to the employer. Staff do not need permission to call as the service is a self referral and there is no limit to how many times a member of staff can call. The service is FREE at point of use.

For more details and for a link to external Care first website go to:  http://www.carefirst-lifestyle.co.uk (Username: gcc001 and Password: uncil1234)  

Once logged in take time to familiarise yourself with the FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions), there is also a DVD to further explain how Care first may support you.


Staff Health and Wellbeing Champions

Health and Wellbeing Champions are a network of volunteers working within GCC who are co-ordinated by Occupational Health. The Champions are members of staff, from across the workforce, all of whom have an interest in health and wellbeing matters and have attended a Health Promotion Course.

The group work as advocates for Health and Wellbeing.

The Champions act as a point of contact for members of staff to promote the health and wellbeing message and to share information which will support employees to adopt and maintain a healthy lifestyle.
