Safeguarding

What you need to know

Here you will find an overview of safeguarding across the Council. If you are specifically working with vulnerable children or adults you will get far more in-depth training through your manager. This is a brief overview for ALL staff, whether you are working with vulnerable people or not.

Gloucestershire County Council’s work is very much concerned with vulnerable people across the County.

Safeguarding – covers the process of promoting welfare and protecting individuals from harm

  • Adult – over 18 years and over
  • Children – not reached 18th Birthday - includes unborn child
