Equalities

 

Beyond legislation

Other work that is undertaken by Gloucestershire
County Council above and beyond the requirements
of the Public Sector Equality duty includes:

  • Positive Action / Positive Discrimination
  • Anti-Discrimination
  • Community Impact Assessments
  • Hate Crime
  • Employee Networks

 

 

The Equalities Agenda strives to achieve the following aims:

  • That staff and customers are treated with dignity and fairness.
  • That people's cultures, beliefs and backgrounds are respected and welcomed.
  • That good practice is evidenced and shared across the County Council
