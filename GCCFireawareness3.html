Fire Awareness

Basic emergency procedures

On your first day your manager should have explained to you:

  • The action to be taken in the event of a fire
  • Where the fire alarm call points (Break Glass) are
  • The exits available from your location
  • Basic extinguisher information
  • Fire drills and alarm tests
  • What the alarm sounds like
  • Basic fire protection/awareness
  • Your assembly point
  • Who your fire warden is

If you don’t know any of these points, make sure you find out from your manager promptly. If you work in the Shire Hall complex the Assembly points and fire wardens can be found in the downloads here

So in the event of an emergency or a drill:

  • Leave the building by the quickest and safest route (make sure you know the alternative exit)
  • Ensure any visitors leave with you
  • Close the doors behind you
  • Do not return for personal belongings
  • Do not attempt to use the lifts
  • Make your way to the designated assembly point
  • Remain there until you are advised on the next course of action.

 

More detail can be found at http://staffnet.gloscc.gov.uk/fireprocedure

Fire extinguishers

For most people extinguishers are provided as an aid to protect the means of escape. The main two types of fire extinguisher around Shire Hall are Water (all red with white writing) and Carbon Dioxide (CO2 – all red with a black label and plastic horn).
However you should read and familiarise yourself with the types of fire extinguisher in your workplace

Further Information

If you want further information about fire awareness or fire safety in the first instance speak to your manager and then your fire warden.

If you need more information about fire training or fire risk assessments details can be found on GCC Web or from jason.gregory@gloucestershire.gov.uk who can provide details of the training available which includes Fire awareness renewal, Fire extinguisher training, PEEP (personnel emergency evacuation plan)  training, responsible person training and fire risk assessment training. This training it can be delivered on site or at Shire hall.

If you have a need for any of the above training please contact us for a tailored costing.

In addition Fire risk assessments are available with the price dependant on the size of the property.
